# Part 17: South Indian Bank & CSB Bank (Fintech Partnerships)

Part 17 focuses on regional banking powerhouses that have achieved national reach by partnering with aggressive fintech startups. **South Indian Bank (SIB)** and **CSB Bank** might not be the first names that come to mind for credit cards, but their Lifetime Free (LTF) co-branded offerings are highly competitive.

---

## 🏦 The Fintech Partnership Model

Instead of building massive IT infrastructure to acquire credit card customers, these banks act as the underlying licensed financial institution, while fintech apps handle the UI, rewards, and customer acquisition.

---

## 💳 Jupiter Edge CSB Bank Credit Card

Jupiter started as a neobank offering zero-balance savings accounts. They expanded into credit with the **Jupiter Edge CSB Bank Credit Card**.

*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free.
*   **Network:** RuPay (UPI enabled).
*   **Key Benefits:**
    *   **Direct Cashback:** Earn up to 2% flat cashback on specific categories (like travel, dining, or shopping) that you choose within the Jupiter app.
    *   **UPI Rewards:** Because it is a RuPay card, you earn reward points even when scanning QR codes at local merchants via the Jupiter app or Google Pay.
    *   **Jewels System:** Rewards are earned as "Jewels," which never expire and can be instantly converted to digital gold or cash into your Jupiter account.

## 💳 South Indian Bank (SIB) Co-Brands

South Indian Bank issues several Lifetime Free cards, most notably backing the **OneCard** (discussed in Part 16).

Additionally, SIB offers its own core **SIB Simply SAVE Credit Card**, which is frequently offered as LTF during festive seasons. It provides basic reward points on dining and online shopping.

---

## 🎯 The TCS Employee Verdict

While an HDFC or ICICI card will provide better premium benefits (like lounge access), the **Jupiter Edge CSB RuPay Card** is an excellent fallback option. 

If you are a TCS employee looking to completely separate your daily UPI expenses (tea, snacks, auto-rickshaw rides) from your main salary account, linking the Jupiter Edge RuPay card to your UPI app keeps your bank statement clean while earning you free cashback.
