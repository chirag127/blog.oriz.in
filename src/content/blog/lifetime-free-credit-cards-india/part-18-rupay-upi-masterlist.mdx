# Part 18: RuPay UPI Lifetime Free Master List

In 2026, the hottest trend in the Indian credit card market is **RuPay Credit Cards on UPI**. The RBI allowed credit cards to be linked to UPI apps (like Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm), allowing you to scan QR codes at the local Kirana store and pay using credit.

To maximize this, you **must** use a Lifetime Free (LTF) card. Paying an annual fee just to earn 1% cashback on a ₹50 tea transaction makes no mathematical sense.

---

## 💳 The RuPay LTF Champions (2026)

Here is the definitive list of RuPay cards that cost absolutely nothing to hold:

### 1. ICICI Coral RuPay Credit Card
*   **Bank:** ICICI Bank (Private)
*   **Status:** Pre-approved LTF for almost all existing ICICI Bank customers.
*   **Strategy:** Open the iMobile app and check your offers. If you have an Amazon Pay card, you can get this as a shared-limit secondary card instantly.

### 2. Tata Neu Plus & Infinity HDFC Bank Cards
*   **Bank:** HDFC Bank (Private)
*   **Status:** Targeted LTF (Especially for TCS / Tata Group employees).
*   **Rewards:** 1.5% NeuCoins on UPI (Infinity) or 1% NeuCoins on UPI (Plus). This is the highest reward rate for UPI in India.

### 3. Jupiter Edge CSB Bank RuPay Card
*   **Bank:** CSB Bank (Private)
*   **Status:** Unconditionally LTF.
*   **Rewards:** Earn Jewels on UPI spends which convert to digital gold or cash.

### 4. YES Bank PaisaSave RuPay Card
*   **Bank:** YES Bank (Private)
*   **Status:** Unconditionally LTF.
*   **Rewards:** Focuses heavily on rewarding small-ticket UPI scans.

### 5. Axis Bank MyZone & Neo RuPay
*   **Bank:** Axis Bank (Private)
*   **Status:** Promotional LTF (Apply only when the Zero Fee banner is active).
*   **Rewards:** Basic UPI rewards, but excellent secondary benefits (SonyLiv, Swiggy).

## ⚠️ Important Rules for UPI on Credit Cards

1.  **Merchant Only (P2M):** You can only scan merchant QR codes (shops, online gateways). You **cannot** send money to a friend's phone number or a personal UPI ID (P2P).
2.  **MDR Charges:** For transactions below ₹2,000, there is usually no charge to the merchant. For transactions above ₹2,000, some small merchants may disable credit card UPI acceptance because they have to pay a 1-2% fee to the bank. 
3.  **TCS Employee Tip:** Use your LTF RuPay card to pay for your daily cafeteria meals, auto-rickshaws, and local groceries. It keeps your salary account statement perfectly clean (making future home loan processing much easier) and builds your CIBIL score for free.
