# Part 8: Federal Bank — Travel & Lifestyle Champions

Federal Bank has made massive inroads into the credit card space largely through highly successful fintech partnerships. They are known for providing excellent Lifetime Free (LTF) options with a strong focus on travel.

---

## 🏦 Federal Bank's Core Policy

*   **Maximum Limit:** Federal Bank typically allows you to hold **one core bank card** (like Celesta or Signet) and **one co-branded card** (like Scapia). 
*   **Tech-Forward:** Their entire application process is highly digitized. Approvals are fast, but they rely heavily on Experian and CIBIL scores.

---

## ✈️ The Fintech Travel King: Scapia Federal Bank Credit Card

Scapia completely disrupted the Indian travel card market. It is a co-branded card managed via the beautifully designed Scapia app, issued in partnership with Federal Bank.

*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free.
*   **Best For:** International Travel & Zero Forex Markup.
*   **Key Benefits:**
    *   **Zero Forex Markup:** Pay exactly the Visa exchange rate when swiping abroad. No hidden 3.5% markup.
    *   **Unlimited Domestic Lounge Access:** You get unlimited access to domestic airport lounges. *Condition:* You must spend a minimum of ₹20,000 in the previous billing cycle to unlock the lounge access for the current cycle.
    *   **Rewards (Scapia Coins):** 10% Scapia coins on all eligible online and offline spends, and 20% on travel bookings done within the Scapia app.
*   **TCS Strategy:** If you are a TCS employee going for an onshore assignment (US, UK, Europe), this is the first card you should apply for. It is free to hold and will save you immense amounts of money on foreign exchange.

---

## 👔 The Core Federal Bank Premium Cards

Federal Bank's core cards are officially priced, but they are incredibly easy to get as Lifetime Free based on your relationship with the bank or through specific application channels.

### 1. Federal Bank Celesta Credit Card
The top-tier card in Federal Bank's core lineup.
*   **Target Audience:** High net worth individuals (usually requires an income of > ₹12 Lakhs/annum).
*   **LTF Status:** Frequently offered LTF to customers holding a premium savings account or via targeted promotional emails.
*   **Benefits:** 
    *   Complimentary domestic airport lounge access (4 per quarter).
    *   Complimentary international airport lounge access (2 per year).
    *   Good reward points structure on dining and entertainment.

### 2. Federal Bank Imperio Credit Card
The mid-tier family and lifestyle card.
*   **LTF Status:** Often LTF based on corporate salary tie-ups.
*   **Benefits:** Accelerated rewards on grocery, health care, and utility bill payments. It also offers domestic lounge access (2 per quarter).

### 3. Federal Bank Signet Credit Card
The entry-level lifestyle card.
*   **LTF Status:** Almost always issued as Lifetime Free to salaried professionals.
*   **Benefits:** Focuses on health and wellness discounts, plus basic reward points.

## Summary for Part 8

Federal Bank is a must-have in your wallet if you travel. The **Scapia Federal Card** is the ultimate zero-forex companion. For TCS employees, acquiring Scapia before an international deployment is a financial no-brainer since it carries absolutely zero maintenance costs.
