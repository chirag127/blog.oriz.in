# Part 11: Bank of Baroda (BOBCARD) — Hidden LTF Gems

Welcome to Part 11. When IT professionals think of premium credit cards, they rarely think of public sector banks. However, **Bank of Baroda (operating its credit card division as BOBCARD)** has quietly built one of the most rewarding card portfolios in India. 

Even better, they aggressively use **Lifetime Free (LTF)** promotions to capture the salaried professional market.

---

## 🏦 BOBCARD's Core Policy

*   **Maximum Limit:** BOBCARD is relatively flexible. You can hold up to **3 primary credit cards**.
*   **Approval Process:** While their card rewards are excellent, their approval process can sometimes be bureaucratic. However, applying digitally (via V-KYC) has streamlined the process significantly in 2026.
*   **The "Promotion" Rule:** BOBCARD's standard pricing involves annual fees. However, almost every quarter, they run a massive campaign offering their entire core lineup as LTF. You must apply *during* these specific promotional windows.

---

## 💳 The Premium Heavyweights

If you apply during a BOBCARD promotional period, you can secure cards that rival HDFC Regalia or ICICI Sapphiro, entirely for free.

### 1. BOBCARD Eterna Credit Card
This is their flagship ultra-premium card. Normally, it carries a ₹2,499 annual fee. But during targeted campaigns (especially leading up to March financial year ends or Diwali), it is offered as LTF to high-income earners.
*   **Target Audience:** Salaried professionals earning > ₹12 Lakhs per annum.
*   **Best For:** Travel and Dining.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   **Unlimited Domestic Lounge Access:** For the primary cardholder AND add-on cardholders.
    *   Accelerated rewards (3.75% value back) on travel, dining, and online shopping.
    *   Lower forex markup (2%).
    *   Buy 1 Get 1 free on movie tickets via Paytm Movies (up to ₹250).
*   **Verdict:** If you see an LTF banner for Eterna, drop everything and apply. Unlimited lounge access on a free card is practically unheard of in 2026.

### 2. BOBCARD Premier Credit Card
The mid-tier travel card.
*   **Benefits:** 5X reward points on travel, dining, and international spends. It offers 1 domestic lounge access per quarter.
*   **Status:** Frequently offered LTF. An excellent backup travel card.

### 3. BOBCARD Select Credit Card
The standard online shopping card.
*   **Benefits:** 5X reward points on online spends, utilities, and dining.
*   **Status:** Almost always offered LTF.

---

## 🛍️ The Co-Branded Specialist

### 4. BOBCARD Snapdeal Credit Card
Even if you don't use Snapdeal often, this co-branded card is famous for one specific reason:
*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free.
*   **Benefit:** 5% unlimited reward points on the Snapdeal app, but more importantly, it gives excellent 1% reward points on regular offline spends, making it a solid baseline card.

### 5. Professional Co-Brands (ICAI, ICSI, CMA)
If you hold professional qualifications (Chartered Accountant, Company Secretary, or Cost Accountant), BOBCARD issues exclusive lifetime free cards (e.g., BOBCARD ICAI). These have excellent reward rates and are permanently free as a gesture of professional goodwill.

---

## 🎯 Corporate Advantage: The TCS Perspective

While TCS employees don't have a direct "Tata Group" advantage with BOBCARD, your salary slip carries immense weight. 

*   **The Strategy:** Do not apply on a random Tuesday. Wait for festivals like Independence Day, Diwali, or Republic Day. BOBCARD runs massive digital campaigns. As an MNC employee, your application will be fast-tracked through V-KYC and you can secure the **Premier** or **Eterna** card LTF instantly.
