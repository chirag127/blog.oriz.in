# Part 3: ICICI Bank — The Unlimited LTF Portfolio

Welcome to Part 3 of our Ultimate Guide. If HDFC Bank is strict about how many cards you can hold, **ICICI Bank** sits on the opposite end of the spectrum. They are incredibly generous with their card issuance, making them a favorite for credit card enthusiasts and IT professionals alike.

---

## 🏦 ICICI Bank's Core Policy: No Hard Limits

Unlike other banks that restrict you to 1 or 3 cards, ICICI Bank has **no publicly stated maximum limit** on the number of primary credit cards you can hold. 
*   **The Policy:** You can hold an Amazon Pay card, a Coral RuPay, a Sapphiro Visa, and a MakeMyTrip card all at the same time. 
*   **The Catch (Shared Limit):** All your ICICI credit cards will share a single, consolidated credit limit. If your total limit is ₹6 Lakhs, that ₹6 Lakhs is distributed across all your ICICI cards.

---

## 💳 The Standard Lifetime Free Cards

These cards are unconditionally lifetime free for the general public, requiring no special corporate ties.

### 1. Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card
As discussed in Part 1, this is the crown jewel of LTF cards.
*   **Best For:** Everything Amazon.
*   **Reward Rate:** 5% cashback for Prime members on Amazon; 2% on Amazon Pay partners; 1% elsewhere.
*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free for everyone.

### 2. ICICI Platinum Chip Credit Card
The most basic credit builder card in the ICICI arsenal.
*   **Best For:** First-time borrowers or students.
*   **Reward Rate:** 2 Reward Points per ₹100 spent (very low value).
*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free.

---

## 🎓 The Corporate & Alumni Advantage: Premium Cards for Free

This is where working for a massive corporation like **TCS**, or graduating from a premier institute, pays massive dividends. ICICI Bank is famous for taking their super-premium paid cards and offering them entirely free to specific "low-risk, high-income" cohorts.

### 3. ICICI Sapphiro Credit Card (Alumni & Corporate Tie-up)
Normally, the Sapphiro card costs a staggering ₹6,500 + GST annually. It is a dual-network card (you get one Amex and one Mastercard/Visa sharing the same limit).

*   **Best For:** Lounge Access, Golf, and Movie Tickets.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   4 Domestic Airport Lounge visits per quarter.
    *   2 International Airport Lounge visits per year (via DreamFolks).
    *   Buy 1 Get 1 Free on movie tickets (up to ₹500) twice a month via BookMyShow.
    *   Up to 4 complimentary Golf rounds/lessons per month.
*   **The LTF Strategy for TCS Employees:**
    1.  **Salary Account Route:** If you are in a senior band at TCS (earning &gt; ₹1.5 - ₹2 Lakhs per month) and hold a Wealth Management salary account with ICICI, the RM will often offer this LTF.
    2.  **The Alumni Route:** Regardless of your current salary, if you graduated from an ICICI-approved list of premier institutes (like IITs, IIMs, NITs, BITS, or top tier medical/engineering colleges), ICICI offers the Sapphiro LTF as part of their "Alumni Program." You just need to upload your degree certificate during the application.

### 4. ICICI Rubyx Credit Card
A tier below Sapphiro, normally costing ₹3,000 + GST annually.
*   **Benefits:** 2 Domestic lounge access per quarter, 2 complimentary railway lounge visits, and Buy 1 Get 1 on movies (up to ₹250).
*   **LTF Strategy:** Similar to Sapphiro, but the salary requirement is much lower. Mid-level TCS employees with ICICI salary accounts are frequently offered the Rubyx LTF as an upgrade from basic cards.

---

## 📱 The Digital Push: RuPay & UPI Integration

### 5. ICICI Coral RuPay Credit Card
The Coral card used to be a paid Visa/Mastercard. However, to capture the UPI market, ICICI Bank has aggressively pushed the RuPay variant as Lifetime Free.
*   **Best For:** Linking to UPI (GPay/PhonePe).
*   **Benefits:** 1 Domestic Lounge access per quarter, and 25% discount on BookMyShow (up to ₹100).
*   **How to get it LTF:** If you already hold an Amazon Pay ICICI card or any other ICICI card, simply log into the **iMobile app**. Look under "Cards &amp; Forex" &gt; "Pre-approved Offers". ICICI issues the Coral RuPay LTF as a secondary card to almost all existing customers instantly.

## Summary for Part 3

ICICI Bank is the easiest bank to deal with if you want a fat wallet without paying a rupee in fees. 
For a TCS employee:
1. Get the **Amazon Pay ICICI** for your shopping.
2. Leverage your degree or salary account to get the **Sapphiro/Rubyx** LTF for movies and lounges.
3. Grab the pre-approved **Coral RuPay** from the iMobile app for your UPI scans.

You now have a powerful 3-card ICICI portfolio costing ₹0.
