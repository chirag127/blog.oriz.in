# Part 5: Kotak Mahindra Bank — The 811 Revolution

Welcome to Part 5. In this section, we cover **Kotak Mahindra Bank**, which revolutionized digital banking in India with its 811 account. Kotak has a mix of unconditional LTF cards (especially for beginners) and targeted LTF cards for salaried professionals.

---

## 🏦 Kotak Mahindra Bank's Core Policy

*   **Maximum Limit:** Kotak generally restricts users to holding a maximum of **2 primary credit cards**.
*   **Shared Limit:** If you hold two Kotak cards, the credit limit is shared between them.
*   **Upgrade Path:** Kotak is notorious for pushing "upgrades" that carry a fee. If you have an LTF card, always double-check the fees before accepting an upgrade offer via their mobile app.

---

## 💳 The Beginner's Champion: Kotak 811 #DreamDifferent

If you are just starting your career, perhaps as a fresh TCS trainee, and you don't have a CIBIL score yet, this is the best lifetime free card to build your credit profile.

*   **Type:** Secured (Fixed Deposit Backed) Credit Card.
*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free.
*   **How it Works:** You open a Kotak 811 digital savings account and create a Fixed Deposit (minimum ₹10,000). The bank issues this credit card against 90% of your FD amount as your credit limit.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   No income proof or CIBIL score required.
    *   You continue to earn interest on your FD (usually around 6-7%).
    *   2 Reward Points per ₹100 spent on online transactions.
    *   1% fuel surcharge waiver.
*   **Verdict:** This is purely a credit-builder card. Once your CIBIL score crosses 750 (usually after 6 months of usage), you can apply for unsecured premium cards.

---

## 🎯 Targeted LTF Cards for IT Professionals

For salaried employees, especially those working in MNCs like TCS, Infosys, and Cognizant, Kotak offers its mid-tier cards as LTF.

### Kotak League Platinum Credit Card
Normally, this card comes with a ₹500 joining fee. However, if you apply through specific corporate channels or hold a salary account with Kotak, it is frequently issued as LTF.

*   **Best For:** General rewards and milestone benefits.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Earn up to 8 Reward Points on every ₹150 spent (on special categories).
    *   Milestone rewards: Free PVR tickets or reward points on crossing ₹1,25,000 in spends per year.
    *   Fuel surcharge waiver.
*   **The TCS Strategy:** If your project mandates a Kotak Salary account, the bank RM will almost certainly offer you the League Platinum (or sometimes the Kotak Mojo) as Lifetime Free. 

---

## 📱 The Co-Branded Push

Kotak also partners with various brands (like Myntra and PVR), but these cards are very rarely offered as Lifetime Free. The **Kotak Myntra Credit Card** is a phenomenal card for fashion shopping (7.5% instant discount), but you will usually have to pay the ₹500 annual fee unless a rare promotional LTF campaign is running.
