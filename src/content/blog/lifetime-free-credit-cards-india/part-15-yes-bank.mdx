# Part 15: YES Bank — The Rewards Resurgence

After a brief period of restructuring a few years ago, **YES Bank** has bounced back aggressively in the credit card space in 2026. To reclaim market share, they have launched a suite of highly rewarding Lifetime Free (LTF) credit cards, particularly focusing on the RuPay network for UPI integration.

---

## 🏦 YES Bank's Core Policy

*   **Aggressive Issuance:** YES Bank is currently one of the easiest banks to get a credit card from if you have a stable salary and a decent CIBIL score.
*   **Limit Sharing:** Like most private banks, holding multiple YES Bank cards means you share a consolidated credit limit.
*   **Subscription Models:** YES Bank is experimenting with "subscription-based" cards, but they still maintain strong pure LTF options.

---

## 💳 The New-Age LTF Portfolio

### 1. YES Bank PaisaSave RuPay Credit Card
Designed specifically for the UPI generation.
*   **Status:** Unconditionally Lifetime Free.
*   **Network:** RuPay (Can be linked to PhonePe, GPay, Paytm).
*   **Best For:** Everyday UPI transactions.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Earn decent cashback/rewards on every UPI scan-and-pay transaction.
    *   Bonus cashback on online shopping and dining.
    *   Fuel surcharge waiver across all pumps.

### 2. YES Bank POP-CLUB Co-branded Credit Card
Partnering with the POP ecosystem, this is a lifestyle card.
*   **Status:** Lifetime Free.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Earn POP Coins on all transactions, which can be redeemed on a wide variety of D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) brands for fashion, grooming, and electronics.

### 3. YES Bank FinBooster Credit Card (with BankBazaar)
A unique credit card that acts as a financial fitness tracker.
*   **Status:** Lifetime Free.
*   **Best For:** Credit tracking and dining.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Free monthly credit score tracking.
    *   Accelerated reward points on online dining (Zomato/Swiggy) and apparel.
    *   Points never expire and can be shared with family members.

---

## 🎯 The TCS Employee Strategy

For a TCS employee, YES Bank offers great utility without the hassle of maintaining a salary account with them. 

The **PaisaSave RuPay** card is the star here. Since RuPay UPI payments are becoming the standard across India, having a dedicated, zero-fee RuPay card from YES Bank ensures your bank account statement stays clean (no more dozens of ₹20 tea transactions cluttering your savings account statement), while earning you free reward points.
