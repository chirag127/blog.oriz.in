# The Ultimate Teen Drama Masterlist: 100 Best Shows to Watch

Welcome to the definitive guide to teen television. Teen dramas are more than just "guilty pleasures"; they are cultural touchstones that explore the messy, beautiful, and high-stakes journey of growing up. 

In this exhaustive 10-part series, we are counting down the **100 best teen dramas of all time**. Whether you're looking for nostalgic 90s angst, supernatural thrills, or gritty modern realism, this series has something for everyone.
