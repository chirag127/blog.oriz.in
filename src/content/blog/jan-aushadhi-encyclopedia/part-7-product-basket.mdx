# Jan Aushadhi Encyclopedia Part 7: The Product Basket — Beyond Medicines

A common misconception is that Jan Aushadhi Kendras only sell basic tablets like Paracetamol. In reality, as of May 2026, the product basket has expanded into a comprehensive healthcare supermarket.

---

## 1. The Medicine Portfolio (2,100+ Items)

The PMBJP covers almost all therapeutic categories found in the National List of Essential Medicines (NLEM).

*   **Chronic Diseases:** High-quality medicines for Diabetes, Hypertension, Cardiac Care, and Cholesterol.
*   **Specialized Drugs:** Anti-cancer drugs, immunosuppressants, and psychiatric medicines.
*   **Acute Care:** Antibiotics, painkillers, cough syrups, and anti-allergics.
*   **Ayurvedic Support:** A growing range of traditional Ayurvedic formulations under the "Jan Aushadhi" brand.

## 2. The Surgical & Device Segment (300+ Items)

You can now find a wide array of medical hardware and consumables at Jan Aushadhi prices:
*   **Diagnostic Tools:** Digital Blood Pressure Monitors, Glucometers, Pulse Oximeters, and Digital Thermometers.
*   **Daily Consumables:** Bandages, Gauze, Cotton, IV sets, and Syringes.
*   **Support Gear:** Knee caps, L.S. Belts (Back support), Cervical collars, and walking sticks.
*   **Nebulizers & Vaporizers:** High-quality electrical nebulizers at nearly 60-70% lower prices than leading brands.

---

## 3. The Suvidha Sanitary Pad Revolution

One of the most impactful products in the Jan Aushadhi basket is the **Janaushadhi Suvidha Sanitary Napkin**.
*   **Price:** Just **₹1 per pad** (Pack of 4 for ₹4).
*   **Eco-Friendly:** These are **Oxo-biodegradable** pads, meaning they break down naturally in the soil, unlike plastic-heavy branded pads.
*   **Impact:** This initiative has significantly improved menstrual hygiene in rural and underprivileged urban areas, where branded pads are an unaffordable luxury.

---

## 4. Nutraceuticals & Wellness

Recognizing the shift toward preventive healthcare, PMBI has introduced a "Wellness" category:
*   **Jan Aushadhi Protein:** High-quality protein powders (including specialized Whey Protein for women).
*   **Multivitamin Supplements:** B-Complex, Zinc, Vitamin D3, and Calcium tablets.
*   **Malt-based Food:** Health drinks for children and elderly.
*   **Nicotine Gum:** Supporting the national tobacco-cessation program.

## 5. "Jan Aushadhi" Brand Quality

All these products are sold under the unified **Jan Aushadhi** brand. This ensures that even for medical devices, the consumer gets the same "NABL Tested" guarantee that they get with medicines.

## Summary

The goal of Jan Aushadhi is to become a **One-Stop Shop** for all healthcare needs. Whether you need a monthly prescription for heart health or a digital BP monitor for your parents, the Kendra provides it all under one roof.
