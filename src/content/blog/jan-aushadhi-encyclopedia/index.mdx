# The Ultimate Jan Aushadhi Encyclopedia

Welcome to the most comprehensive digital resource on the **Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP)**. In a country where healthcare expenses can push families into poverty, the Jan Aushadhi scheme stands as a revolutionary pillar of affordability and quality.

This 12-part series is designed to provide complete, end-to-end information for patients, healthcare professionals, and budding entrepreneurs.
