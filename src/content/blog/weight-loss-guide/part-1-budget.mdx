# 💊 Part 1: Budget Weight Loss Medicines in India
## Medicines Costing ₹2–₹15 Per Tablet

*[← Back to Main Index](/blog/weight-loss-guide)*

These medicines were not made for weight loss — but they are the most accessible, safest, and cheapest tools available in India today. Most are available at any Jan Aushadhi or local chemist.

## 1. 🏆 Metformin (Metformin Hydrochloride)

**Salt Name:** Metformin Hydrochloride  
**Indian Brands:** Glycomet, Glucophage, Obimet, Gluformin, Carbophage  
**Class:** Biguanide / Anti-Diabetic  
**Prescription Required:** Yes  
**Online Availability in India:** ✅ Available (1mg, Apollo, PharmEasy, Netmeds)

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Metformin lowers blood insulin levels and increases insulin sensitivity. High insulin = constant fat storage. Lower insulin = your body can finally burn stored fat. It also mildly suppresses appetite by increasing GLP-1 naturally and affecting hunger hormones.

### Dosage for Weight Loss
- Starting dose: 500mg once daily with dinner
- Therapeutic: 500mg–1000mg twice daily
- Maximum: 2500mg/day (divided doses)

**Monthly Cost (1000mg/day):** ₹60–₹200

### ✅ Benefits
- Reduces insulin resistance
- May have anti-aging properties (being studied for longevity)
- Safe for very long-term use
- Reduces PCOS-related weight gain
- Cardioprotective
- May reduce cancer risk (emerging data)

### ⚠️ Side Effects
| Side Effect | Frequency | Severity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Diarrhea, loose stools | Very Common (20-30%) | Mild |
| Nausea, vomiting | Common | Mild |
| Metallic taste | Common | Mild |
| Vitamin B12 deficiency | Common (long-term) | Moderate — supplement! |
| Lactic Acidosis | Very Rare (\<1 in 30,000) | **Potentially Fatal** |
| Hypoglycemia | Rare (alone) | Low risk |

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Never take Metformin if you have kidney disease (eGFR \<30), liver disease, or are drinking heavily. Lactic acidosis risk is real in these groups.

### 🔬 Expected Weight Loss
- **1–3 kg** over 6–12 months
- Best results when combined with a low-carb diet
- Works better for insulin-resistant individuals (PCOS, pre-diabetes)

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 9/10 for Value. ✅ HIGHLY RECOMMENDED.**  
Metformin is the safest, cheapest, and most studied metabolic drug in existence. Even if you lose only 2 kg, the cardiovascular and longevity benefits are enormous. Start here.

## 2. Acarbose (Alpha-Glucosidase Inhibitor)

**Salt Name:** Acarbose  
**Indian Brands:** Glucobay, Rebose, Acarbose-25, Carbose  
**Class:** Alpha-Glucosidase Inhibitor / Anti-Diabetic  
**Prescription Required:** Yes  
**Online Availability in India:** ✅ Available

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Acarbose blocks the enzyme that breaks down complex carbohydrates in your gut. This delays glucose absorption, blunting post-meal blood sugar and insulin spikes. Lower insulin = less fat storage. Also reduces total calorie absorption slightly.

### Dosage
- Start: 25mg once with first bite of meal
- Target: 50mg–100mg three times daily with meals

### Price in India (April 2026)
| Formulation | Price per Strip (10 tabs) | Price per Tablet |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Acarbose 25mg | ₹60–₹100 | ₹6–₹10 |
| Acarbose 50mg | ₹90–₹140 | ₹9–₹14 |
| Acarbose 100mg | ₹120–₹200 | ₹12–₹20 |

**Monthly Cost (50mg TDS):** ₹270–₹420

### ⚠️ Side Effects
| Side Effect | Frequency | Severity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extreme flatulence (gas)** | **Very Common (70%+)** | Embarrassing |
| Bloating, abdominal cramps | Very Common | Moderate |
| Diarrhea | Common | Mild–Moderate |
| Liver enzyme elevation | Rare (high doses) | Monitor |

> ⚠️ **Warning:** The gas is *extreme*. Most people cannot tolerate this drug socially without drastically reducing carbohydrate intake. Not suitable for high-carb diets.

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 6/10. ⚖️ MAYBE.**  
Works well on paper. Terrible in social situations. Consider only if you can commit to a low-carb diet simultaneously.

## 3. Fluoxetine (Prozac)

**Salt Name:** Fluoxetine Hydrochloride  
**Indian Brands:** Fludac, Prodep, Flunil, Oleanz (combo)  
**Class:** SSRI (Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor) / Antidepressant  
**Prescription Required:** Yes (Schedule H)  
**Online Availability in India:** ✅ Available (prescription required)

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Fluoxetine increases serotonin, which is a satiety signal. This reduces emotional eating and binge-eating impulses. FDA-approved specifically for **Bulimia Nervosa** at higher doses (60mg/day). Weight loss effect is strongest in the first 6 months, then often reverses.

### Dosage
- Depression dose: 20mg/day
- Bulimia/weight loss: 60mg/day (off-label)

### Price in India (April 2026)
| Formulation | Price per Strip (10 caps) | Price per Capsule |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Fluoxetine 10mg | ₹20–₹40 | ₹2–₹4 |
| Fluoxetine 20mg | ₹25–₹60 | ₹2.5–₹6 |
| Fluoxetine 40mg | ₹50–₹100 | ₹5–₹10 |

**Monthly Cost (20mg/day):** ₹75–₹180

### ⚠️ Side Effects
- Insomnia (common, especially at start)
- Sexual dysfunction (decreased libido, anorgasmia)
- Nausea (first 2 weeks)
- **Suicidal ideation** (rare, mainly in under-25s — **FDA Black Box Warning**)
- Long-term: weight gain can reverse (common)

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 5/10. ⚖️ MAYBE (for emotional eaters only).**  
If your weight gain is driven by depression or binge eating, this can help. If you're metabolically healthy, don't bother — the effect reverses.

## 4. Sertraline (Zoloft)

**Salt Name:** Sertraline Hydrochloride  
**Indian Brands:** Zosert, Serlift, Daxid, Tralin  
**Prescription Required:** Yes

### Weight Loss Role
Early-phase SSRI use (first 3-6 months) can suppress appetite slightly. However, **sertraline frequently causes long-term weight gain** and is NOT a useful weight loss tool.

**Price:** ₹4–₹30 per tablet (50mg)  
**Verdict: ❌ NO.** Weight neutral to weight-gaining long-term. Skip for weight loss purposes.

## 5. Chromium Picolinate

**Salt Name:** Chromium Picolinate  
**Brands:** HealthVit, NOW Foods, Himalaya  
**Class:** Mineral Supplement / OTC  
**Prescription Required:** No  
**Online Availability:** ✅ Amazon, Healthkart, Flipkart

### How it Works
Chromium enhances the action of insulin, reducing sugar cravings and improving glucose metabolism. Most effective in people with chromium deficiency (common in insulin-resistant individuals).

**Price:** ₹200–₹600 for 60 tablets  
**Per Tablet:** ₹3–₹10

### ⚠️ Side Effects
- Generally very safe
- High doses (>1000mcg/day): kidney and liver strain (rare)
- Headaches at high doses

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 7/10. ✅ YES (for insulin resistance).**  
Cheap, safe, and effective for reducing sugar cravings. Great addition to a Metformin stack.
