## 🧠 The Science: Why Weight Loss is Biological, Not Just Willpower

Most obesity is driven by:
- **Hyperinsulinemia**: Too much insulin → constant fat storage
- **Leptin resistance**: Your brain doesn't "feel" full
- **Dopamine dysregulation**: Food becomes addictive
- **Thyroid/Cortisol imbalance**: Metabolic rate drops

Medicines target these root causes. Exercise and diet alone often fail because they don't fix the underlying hormonal dysregulation.
