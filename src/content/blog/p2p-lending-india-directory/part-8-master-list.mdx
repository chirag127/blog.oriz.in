## 📜 Closing Thoughts
The Indian P2P market is currently in a "consolidation phase." While there are over 50 names associated with the industry, the top 10 platforms facilitate over 90% of the volume. 

**Pro Tip**: Always look for the **"NBFC-P2P"** tag in the footer of any app you use. If it's not there, they are likely not an RBI-regulated peer-to-peer entity.
