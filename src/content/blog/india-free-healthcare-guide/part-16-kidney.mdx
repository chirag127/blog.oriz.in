# Part 16: Free Kidney Dialysis & Transplant

Kidney disease requires lifelong dialysis (₹2,000-4,000 per session, 3x/week = ₹2.5-5 Lakh/year) or transplant (₹5-15 Lakh). Here's how to get it free.

---

## 1. Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Programme (PMNDP)
*   **Free dialysis** at designated government hospitals across India.
*   Operated through PPP (Public-Private Partnership) model — private operators run dialysis units inside government hospitals.
*   **Eligibility:** BPL families get free. Others may pay subsidized rates.
*   **Available at:** Most District Hospitals and Medical Colleges.

## 2. Ayushman Bharat Dialysis Coverage
*   PM-JAY covers dialysis sessions as part of its packages.
*   Cashless at empanelled hospitals.

## 3. Best Government Nephrology Centres
*   **PGIMER, Chandigarh** — India's best for nephrology and renal transplant.
*   **AIIMS New Delhi** — Advanced nephrology and transplant.
*   **SGPGIMS, Lucknow** — Excellent nephrology department.
*   **CMC Vellore** — World-class (semi-govt/charitable). Subsidized for poor.

## 4. Kidney Transplant under PM-JAY
*   Living donor kidney transplant is covered under PM-JAY.
*   Package rate: ~₹2.5-3 Lakh (vs ₹8-15 Lakh private).
*   Post-transplant immunosuppressive drugs need long-term management — check if covered under your state scheme.
