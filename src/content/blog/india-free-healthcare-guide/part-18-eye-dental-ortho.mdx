# Part 18: Free Eye Care, Dental & Orthopaedic Treatment

## Free Eye Care

### National Programme for Control of Blindness (NPCBVI)
*   **Free cataract surgery** at government hospitals and eye camps.
*   India performs 6+ million cataract surgeries per year — the world's highest volume.
*   **Free spectacles** distributed through school screening programmes.
*   **Available at:** District hospitals, Regional Eye Centres, and outreach camps.

### Best Government Eye Hospitals:
*   **AIIMS (all campuses)** — Ophthalmology departments.
*   **RP Centre for Ophthalmic Sciences, AIIMS Delhi** — India's best.
*   **Shankar Nethralaya, Chennai** — Semi-charitable. Subsidized.
*   **LV Prasad Eye Institute** — Hyderabad, Bhubaneswar. Free for 50%+ patients.

### PM-JAY Eye Packages:
*   Cataract surgery with IOL: Covered cashless.
*   Retinal detachment repair: Covered.
*   Glaucoma surgery: Covered.

## Free Dental Care

*   **Government dental colleges** provide free/₹10 OPD dental treatment.
*   Available at every government medical college and many district hospitals.
*   Services: Extraction, filling, cleaning, root canal (at teaching hospitals), dentures.

## Free Orthopaedic Care

### Fractures & Trauma:
*   Free at all government hospital emergency departments.
*   Plating, casting, traction — all free at government hospitals.

### Joint Replacement under PM-JAY:
*   **Knee replacement:** Package rate ~₹80,000-1,20,000 (vs ₹3-5 Lakh private).
*   **Hip replacement:** Package rate ~₹80,000-1,20,000.
*   Cashless at empanelled hospitals.
