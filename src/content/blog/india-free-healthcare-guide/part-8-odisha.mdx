# Part 8: Odisha — Where Healthcare is Truly Free

Odisha has quietly built one of India's most comprehensive free healthcare ecosystems. The combination of BSKY + Niramaya + NIDAN means almost everything is free at state hospitals.

## Key Schemes:
*   **BSKY:** ₹5 Lakh/family (₹10 Lakh for women). Cashless at govt + private hospitals.
*   **Niramaya:** Free medicines (500+ drugs) at all government hospital DDCs.
*   **NIDAN:** Free diagnostics (CT, MRI, X-ray, all lab tests) at government hospitals.
*   Combined result: **OPD + Medicines + Tests + Surgery + ICU = ₹0** at state hospitals.

## Top Government Hospitals:
*   **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** — Central govt. 40+ departments. Emergency free.
*   **Capital Hospital (PGIMER)** — State flagship. Everything free.
*   **SCB Medical College, Cuttack** — Largest medical college in Odisha. All specialties.
*   **KIMS, Bhubaneswar** — Private but BSKY-empanelled. Cashless.
*   **VIMSAR, Burla** — Major govt medical college for Western Odisha.
