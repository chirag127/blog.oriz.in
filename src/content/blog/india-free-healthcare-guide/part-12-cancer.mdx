# Part 12: Free Cancer Treatment in India

Cancer treatment can cost ₹5-50 Lakh in private hospitals. But you can get it for **free or near-free** at these institutions.

---

## Top Centres for Free/Subsidized Cancer Treatment

### 1. Tata Memorial Centre (TMC), Mumbai — India's #1
*   Has a "General" category system. If assessed as unable to pay → treatment is **free/heavily subsidized**.
*   World-class oncology: surgery, chemo, radiation, BMT.
*   Medical Social Worker (MSW) department helps patients navigate financial aid.
*   Network hospitals in Varanasi, Sangrur, Visakhapatnam, Guwahati.

### 2. AIIMS New Delhi — National Cancer Institute
*   Advanced cancer care including proton therapy.
*   Subsidized for all. Free emergency.
*   OPD: ₹10 registration.

### 3. Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology, Bengaluru
*   Government-supported. High-volume, affordable cancer care.
*   BPL patients: free treatment.

### 4. Cancer Institute (WIA), Adyar, Chennai
*   Charitable institution. Free treatment for a large % of patients.

### 5. Regional Cancer Centres (RCCs)
*   **Thiruvananthapuram (Kerala)** — One of India's best.
*   **Kolkata (CNCI)** — Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute.
*   **Ahmedabad (GCRI)** — Gujarat Cancer Research Institute.
*   **Guwahati** — Cachar Cancer Hospital.

## Government Financial Aid for Cancer Patients

| Scheme | Coverage | Who Can Apply |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)** | ₹5 Lakh cashless | SECC beneficiaries |
| **Health Minister's Cancer Patient Fund (HMCPF)** | Financial assistance | BPL patients at 27 RCCs |
| **Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi (RAN)** | One-time financial aid | Poor patients at govt hospitals |
| **PM National Relief Fund (PMNRF)** | Case-by-case grants | Any Indian citizen |
| **CM Relief Fund (CMRF)** | State-level financial aid | State residents |

## How to Get Free Cancer Treatment:
1.  **Get diagnosed** at a government hospital (free/cheap).
2.  **Go to a Tata Memorial/RCC.** Meet the Medical Social Worker.
3.  **Submit income documents** — income certificate, Aadhaar, BPL card.
4.  **Get classified** as "No Charge" or "Subsidized" category.
5.  **If eligible for PM-JAY**, use your Ayushman card for cashless treatment.
