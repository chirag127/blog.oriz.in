# Part 17: Free Maternity, Child Health & Women's Healthcare

India has the most extensive maternal and child health programme network in the world. Here is every scheme.

---

## National Schemes for Mothers

### JSSK (Janani Shishu Suraksha Karyakram) — Everything Free
*   **Free delivery** (normal + C-section) at all government hospitals.
*   **Free medicines, tests, blood transfusion, food** during hospital stay.
*   **Free transport** (102 ambulance to and from hospital).
*   **Free newborn care** for 30 days after birth.

### JSY (Janani Suraksha Yojana) — Cash Incentive
*   ₹1,400 (rural) / ₹1,000 (urban) cash incentive for institutional delivery.
*   Directly into bank account of the mother.

### PMMVY (Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana)
*   ₹6,000 (first child) or ₹5,000 (subsequent births) cash benefit.
*   Paid in installments during pregnancy and after delivery.
*   Apply through Anganwadi worker or online at pmmvy.wcd.gov.in.

## National Schemes for Children

### RBSK (Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram)
*   Free health screening for all children 0-18 years.
*   **30 identified conditions** treated completely free: congenital heart defects, cleft lip, club foot, hearing aids, spectacles, childhood cancers.

### UIP (Universal Immunization Programme)
*   **All vaccines free** at government facilities: BCG, OPV, Pentavalent, Rotavirus, MR, JE, DPT booster, PCV.

### ICDS (Integrated Child Development Services)
*   Free supplementary nutrition for children under 6.
*   Delivered through 14 Lakh+ Anganwadi centres across India.
