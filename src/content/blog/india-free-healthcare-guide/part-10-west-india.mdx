# Part 10: Maharashtra, Gujarat & Western India

## Maharashtra:
*   **Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY):** ₹1.5-2 Lakh coverage (converged with PM-JAY for higher limit). Cashless at empanelled hospitals.
*   **Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai** — India's #1 cancer hospital. BPL patients get near-free treatment. "General" category = heavily subsidized.
*   **KEM Hospital, Mumbai** — Premier govt hospital. Free OPD. Excellent for surgery.
*   **JJ Hospital, Mumbai** — Large government hospital. Free for BPL.
*   **Sassoon Hospital, Pune** — Major govt hospital for Pune region.
*   **AIIMS Nagpur** — Neurosurgery, general medicine.

## Gujarat:
*   **Mukhyamantri Amrutum (MA) Yojana:** ₹5 Lakh coverage. Cashless at empanelled hospitals.
*   **Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad** — Asia's largest hospital by bed count (~2,500 beds). Free OPD.
*   **Gujarat Cancer Research Institute (GCRI), Ahmedabad** — Free/subsidized cancer treatment.
*   **AIIMS Rajkot** — Newer AIIMS campus.

## Goa:
*   Goa Medical College, Bambolim — Free OPD and treatment for Goa residents.
