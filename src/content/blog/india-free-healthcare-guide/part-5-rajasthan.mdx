# Part 5: Rajasthan — India's Most Generous Healthcare Coverage

Rajasthan stands out as having **India's highest state health insurance coverage** at up to **₹25 Lakh per family per year** under the Chiranjeevi/Ayushman Arogya Yojana, plus additional accident cover of ₹10 Lakh.

---

## Key Schemes
*   **Mukhyamantri Chiranjeevi Swasthya Bima Yojana (now Ayushman Arogya):** ₹25 Lakh coverage per family, cashless at govt + private hospitals. Additional ₹10 Lakh accident cover for family head.
*   **Mukhyamantri Nishulk Dava Yojana (Free Medicine):** 700+ essential drugs free at all government hospitals since 2011.
*   **Mukhyamantri Nishulk Jaanch Yojana (Free Diagnostics):** Free CT, MRI, X-ray, blood tests at government hospitals.

## Top Government Hospitals
*   **SMS Hospital, Jaipur** — Largest government hospital in Rajasthan. All specialties.
*   **AIIMS Jodhpur** — Central government. Neurology, orthopaedics.
*   **SN Medical College, Jodhpur** — Major state medical college.
*   **JLN Hospital, Ajmer** — Large district-level facility.
*   **Acharya Tulsi Regional Cancer Centre, Bikaner** — Free cancer treatment for BPL.

## Why Rajasthan Ranks High:
₹25 Lakh coverage is 5x the national Ayushman Bharat limit. Combined with free medicines and diagnostics, it's arguably **India's best overall state healthcare package**.
