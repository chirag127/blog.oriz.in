## 🏥 The Big Picture

India's public healthcare system operates on three levels:
1. **Central Government** — AIIMS (23 across India), PGIMER, JIPMER, NIMHANS, ESI hospitals.
2. **State Government** — Medical colleges, district hospitals, CHCs, PHCs.
3. **Insurance Schemes** — Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) + state schemes (BSKY, CMCHIS, Chiranjeevi, KASP).

**If you know how to navigate this system, you can get world-class healthcare for ₹0.**
