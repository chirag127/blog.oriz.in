# Part 1: Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) — India's Biggest Health Insurance

**Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY)** is the world's largest government-funded health insurance scheme. It provides **₹5 Lakh per family per year** for free, cashless treatment at any empanelled hospital in India.

---

## 1. Coverage at a Glance

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Annual Coverage** | ₹5,00,000 per family |
| **Premium** | ₹0 (Government pays) |
| **Beneficiaries** | ~55 Crore Indians (~40% of population) |
| **Hospitals** | 30,000+ empanelled (govt + private) |
| **Packages** | 1,900+ medical/surgical procedures |
| **Pre-existing Diseases** | Covered from Day 1 |
| **Family Size Limit** | No limit |
| **Age Limit** | No limit |
| **Portability** | Can use in any state |

## 2. What's Covered?
*   **All surgeries** — Cardiac bypass, joint replacement, cancer surgery, brain surgery, kidney transplant.
*   **Cancer treatment** — Chemotherapy, radiation therapy, surgical oncology.
*   **Cardiac care** — Angioplasty, pacemaker, valve replacement.
*   **Dialysis** — Kidney dialysis sessions.
*   **Maternity** — Normal delivery, C-section.
*   **Mental health** — Inpatient psychiatric care (select packages).
*   **Pre-hospitalization** — 3 days before admission (tests, medicines).
*   **Post-hospitalization** — 15 days after discharge (follow-up, medicines).
*   **Day-care procedures** — Cataract surgery, chemotherapy sessions, dialysis.

## 3. Who is Eligible?

### Automatically Eligible:
*   Families identified in the **SECC 2011** (Socio-Economic Caste Census) database.
*   **Rural criteria:** Families with kutcha house, no adult earning member, female-headed households, SC/ST families, landless labourers, manual scavengers, destitute, beggars.
*   **Urban criteria:** Rag pickers, street vendors, domestic workers, construction workers, plumbers, painters, security guards, coolies, rickshaw pullers, sweepers, etc.

### Special Category (2024 Expansion):
*   **ALL citizens aged 70+ years** are now eligible regardless of income or existing insurance.

### NOT Eligible:
*   Income-tax payers, government employees (CGHS/ESI covers them), families not in the SECC database.

## 4. How to Check Eligibility & Get Your Card

### Step 1: Check Online
*   Go to [beneficiary.nha.gov.in](https://beneficiary.nha.gov.in)
*   Enter your mobile number → OTP → Search by Aadhaar, ration card, or name.

### Step 2: Get Your Golden Card
*   **Online:** Complete e-KYC on the portal using Aadhaar OTP or face authentication. Download instantly.
*   **Offline:** Visit any **Common Service Centre (CSC)** or the **Ayushman Mitra desk** at empanelled hospitals. Carry Aadhaar + ration card.

### Step 3: Use It
*   Walk into any empanelled hospital → Show your Ayushman card + Aadhaar → Treatment is cashless.

## 5. Helplines

*   **Toll-Free:** **1800-111-565**
