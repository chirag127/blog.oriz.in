# Part 6: Kerala KASP — The Gold Standard of Public Healthcare

Kerala consistently ranks **#1 in India** on health indices (infant mortality, life expectancy, maternal health). Its public healthcare system is a model for the nation.

## Key Scheme: KASP (Karunya Arogya Suraksha Padhathi)
*   **Coverage:** ₹5 Lakh per family (converged with PM-JAY).
*   **Portability:** Can use across India at empanelled hospitals.
*   **Pre-existing diseases:** Covered from Day 1.

## What Makes Kerala Special:
*   **Palliative Care:** Kerala has India's best palliative care network. Free home-based palliative care for terminal patients.
*   **Primary Healthcare:** PHCs and CHCs are well-equipped and well-staffed. You can get quality care even at a village-level PHC.
*   **Mental Health:** Strong district mental health programme. Active Tele-MANAS integration.
*   **Literacy & Awareness:** 96%+ literacy means people know their rights and demand better services.

## Top Government Hospitals:
*   **Government Medical College, Thiruvananthapuram** — Largest govt hospital in Kerala.
*   **Government Medical College, Kozhikode (Calicut)** — Excellent cardiology and oncology.
*   **Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute, Trivandrum** — Central govt. India's best for neurology and cardiac surgery.
*   **Regional Cancer Centre (RCC), Trivandrum** — One of India's top 5 cancer centres. Free for BPL.
