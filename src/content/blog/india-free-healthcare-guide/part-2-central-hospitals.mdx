# Part 2: Central Government Hospitals — India's Best

Central government hospitals are the **gold standard** of public healthcare in India. Funded by the Union Government, they have the best doctors, equipment, and infrastructure. Here is a guide to the most important ones.

---

## The AIIMS Network (23 Institutions)

AIIMS institutions are the crown jewels. All are open to **every Indian citizen** regardless of state.

| AIIMS Campus | Location | Best Known For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AIIMS New Delhi** | Delhi | India's #1 hospital. All specialties. Trauma. |
| **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** | Odisha | Cancer, Cardiology, Emergency |
| **AIIMS Patna** | Bihar | Trauma, General Medicine |
| **AIIMS Jodhpur** | Rajasthan | Neurology, Orthopaedics |
| **AIIMS Rishikesh** | Uttarakhand | Trauma, Emergency |
| **AIIMS Bhopal** | MP | Cardiology, Gastro |
| **AIIMS Raipur** | Chhattisgarh | General Surgery |
| **AIIMS Mangalagiri** | Andhra Pradesh | Cancer, Nephrology |
| **AIIMS Nagpur** | Maharashtra | Neurosurgery |
| **AIIMS Kalyani** | West Bengal | General Medicine |

**OPD at all AIIMS:** ₹10 registration (valid 1 year). Emergency: Free.

---

## Other Premier Central Institutions

### PGIMER Chandigarh
*   **Best for:** Nephrology, Hepatology, Neurosurgery, Ophthalmology.
*   **OPD:** ₹30 registration.
*   **Why it matters:** Consistently ranked #2 in India after AIIMS Delhi.

### JIPMER Puducherry
*   **Best for:** Cardiology, Paediatrics, Obstetrics, General Medicine.
*   **OPD:** Free.
*   **Why it matters:** One of the few institutions where OPD is 100% free.

### NIMHANS Bengaluru
*   **Best for:** Mental Health, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Psychiatry, Epilepsy, De-addiction.
*   **OPD:** ₹10 registration.
*   **Why it matters:** India's #1 mental health institution. If you have depression, bipolar disorder, or epilepsy, this is the best place.

### Safdarjung Hospital, Delhi
*   **Best for:** Burns, Plastic Surgery, Orthopaedics, Emergency.
*   **OPD:** Free. Emergency: Free.

### RML Hospital, Delhi
*   **Best for:** General Medicine, Surgery.
*   **OPD:** ₹10. Emergency: Free.

### Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai
*   **Best for:** Cancer (India's #1 cancer hospital).
*   **Costs:** Heavily subsidized. BPL patients get free treatment.

---

## Key Takeaway

Central government hospitals are open to **ALL Indians**. You do NOT need to be from that state. If you have a complex medical condition, a central institution should be your first choice.
