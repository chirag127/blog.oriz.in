# Part 19: Which State Has the Best Free Healthcare? The Ranking

Based on health insurance coverage, free medicine/diagnostic schemes, hospital infrastructure, and health outcomes, here is our ranking of Indian states for free public healthcare.

## Why Rajasthan Ranks #1:
*   **₹25 Lakh coverage** is unmatched in India (5x national average).
*   **Free medicine scheme since 2011** — one of the oldest and most established.
*   **Free diagnostic scheme** — CT, MRI, X-ray, all lab tests.
*   **Additional ₹10 Lakh accident cover.**

## Why Kerala Ranks #2:
*   Best **health outcomes** in India (lowest infant mortality, highest life expectancy).
*   Best **primary healthcare** network.
*   Best **palliative care** system.
*   Highest healthcare **awareness** due to literacy.

## Key Insights:
*   **South India** (Kerala, TN, Karnataka, Odisha) generally has better public healthcare than North India.
*   **Delhi** punches above its weight because of the concentration of central hospitals.
*   **UP, Bihar, MP** lag behind due to weak infrastructure despite PM-JAY coverage.
*   **For mental health specifically:** Karnataka (NIMHANS) > Jharkhand (CIP) > all others.
