# Part 7: Tamil Nadu — South India's Best Public Healthcare System

Tamil Nadu pioneered many of India's healthcare innovations: centralized drug procurement (TNMSC), 108 ambulance services, and comprehensive health insurance.

## Key Scheme: CMCHIS (Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance)
*   **Coverage:** ₹5 Lakh per family (converged with PM-JAY).
*   **Speciality:** Covers 1,000+ procedures including critical illnesses.
*   **Cashless** at government + empanelled private hospitals.

## Why Tamil Nadu Excels:
*   **TNMSC (Tamil Nadu Medical Services Corporation):** India's best drug procurement system. Ensures medicines are always in stock at government hospitals.
*   **108 Ambulance:** Tamil Nadu pioneered the 108 emergency ambulance service.
*   **Government hospitals are well-maintained** — Tamil Nadu invests heavily in government hospital infrastructure.

## Top Government Hospitals:
*   **Government General Hospital (Rajiv Gandhi), Chennai** — One of India's oldest and largest hospitals. Free OPD.
*   **Madras Medical College, Chennai** — Premier medical college. All specialties.
*   **JIPMER, Puducherry** — Central govt. 100% free OPD.
*   **Cancer Institute (WIA), Adyar, Chennai** — Charitable. Free treatment for poor patients.
*   **Government Stanley Hospital, Chennai** — Excellent for surgery and trauma.
*   **AIIMS Madurai** — Newest AIIMS in Tamil Nadu.
