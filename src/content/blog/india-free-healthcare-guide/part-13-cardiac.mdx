# Part 13: Free Cardiac Care — Heart Surgery for ₹0

Heart surgery can cost ₹3-15 Lakh in private hospitals. Here's how to get it free or near-free.

---

## Top Centres for Free/Subsidized Cardiac Care

| Hospital | Location | Specialties | Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AIIMS New Delhi** | Delhi | Bypass, valve, angioplasty | ₹10 OPD. Surgery subsidized. |
| **GB Pant Hospital** | Delhi | Cardiac surgery | Free/subsidized |
| **Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute** | Trivandrum | Cardiac surgery, interventional cardiology | Subsidized |
| **PGIMER** | Chandigarh | Cardiac surgery | Subsidized (₹30 OPD) |
| **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** | Odisha | Cardiology, CTVS | ₹10 OPD |
| **KIMS** | Bhubaneswar | Cardiac care | Free with BSKY |
| **Madras Medical College** | Chennai | Cardiac surgery | Free at govt hospital |

## Ayushman Bharat Cardiac Packages

Under PM-JAY, the following are covered **cashless** at empanelled hospitals:
*   **Coronary angioplasty (single stent):** Package covers up to ₹80,000-1,20,000
*   **CABG (Bypass surgery):** Package covers up to ₹1,70,000-2,50,000
*   **Valve replacement:** Package covers up to ₹1,50,000-2,00,000
*   **Pacemaker implantation:** Package covers up to ₹50,000-80,000
*   **Diagnostic angiography:** Package covers up to ₹12,000-15,000

## How to Get Free Heart Surgery:
1.  **Get an ECG + Echo** at your nearest government hospital (free under NIDAN/state schemes).
2.  **If surgery is needed**, get referred to AIIMS, PGIMER, or a medical college.
3.  **Use PM-JAY** for cashless surgery at empanelled private hospitals.
4.  **If not PM-JAY eligible**, government hospital surgery is heavily subsidized (₹5,000-15,000 vs ₹5-15 Lakh private).
