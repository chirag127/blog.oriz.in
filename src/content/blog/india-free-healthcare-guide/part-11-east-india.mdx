# Part 11: West Bengal, Northeast & Eastern India

## West Bengal:
*   **Swasthya Sathi:** ₹5 Lakh coverage per family. Cashless at govt + empanelled private hospitals. Covers all families (universal, not income-based).
*   **SSKM Hospital, Kolkata** — Premier govt hospital. All specialties.
*   **NRS Medical College, Kolkata** — Large teaching hospital. Free OPD.
*   **Bangur Institute, Kolkata** — Best for neurology and neurosurgery in East India.
*   **Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute, Kolkata** — Free/subsidized cancer treatment.
*   **AIIMS Kalyani** — Newest AIIMS in Bengal.

## Bihar:
*   **AIIMS Patna** — Central govt. Trauma and emergency.
*   **PMCH (Patna Medical College)** — Largest state hospital.
*   State scheme converged with PM-JAY.

## Jharkhand:
*   **CIP Ranchi (Central Institute of Psychiatry)** — India's #2 mental health institution after NIMHANS. Free/subsidized psychiatric care.
*   **RIMS Ranchi** — Major govt medical college.

## Northeast:
*   **LGBRIMH, Tezpur, Assam** — Regional mental health institute. Free psychiatric care.
*   **GMCH Guwahati** — Premier govt hospital of Assam.
*   **AIIMS Guwahati** — Under development.
*   **NEIGRIHMS, Shillong** — Central govt. The AIIMS of Northeast.
