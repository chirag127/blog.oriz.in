# Part 7: Hospitals Near Niladri Vihar — A Local Map

If you live in **Niladri Vihar** (Chandrasekharpur area), here is a hyperlocal guide to every healthcare facility within easy reach.

---

## Within 5 km

### 1. Kalinga Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~4 km (Chandrasekharpur)
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty | BSKY: ✅
*   **Best for:** Emergency, general surgery, orthopaedics.

### 2. KIMS Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~5 km (Patia, KIIT Campus)
*   **Type:** Private, Super-specialty | BSKY: ✅
*   **Best for:** Cardiac, neuro, cancer, and complex cases.

### 3. Apollo Clinic / Sparsh Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~3-4 km (various locations in Chandrasekharpur)
*   **Type:** Private Clinic / Small Hospital
*   **Best for:** OPD consultations, minor procedures, lab tests.

### 4. Local Polyclinics & Nursing Homes
Several small nursing homes and polyclinics operate within the Niladri Vihar and Sailashree Vihar area:
*   **Best for:** Fever, cough, cold, routine blood tests, pregnancy check-ups.
*   **Note:** These are NOT free. They charge private rates. Use them only for convenience when government hospitals are too far.

---

## 5-10 km Range

### 5. IMS and SUM Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~6 km (Ghatikia)
*   **Type:** Private, Super-specialty | BSKY: ✅

### 6. Hi-Tech Medical College & Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~7 km (Rasulgarh/Pandara)
*   **Type:** Government Medical College | Free Treatment: ✅

### 7. Capital Hospital (PGIMER)
*   **Distance:** ~8 km (Unit-6)
*   **Type:** State Government Flagship | Free Treatment: ✅

### 8. ESIC Model Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~7 km (Nayapalli)
*   **Type:** Central Government (for ESI-insured employees) | Free for ESI members: ✅

*   **Type:** Central Government, Super-specialty | Subsidized Treatment

### 10. AMRI Hospital
*   **Distance:** ~12 km (Khandagiri)
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty | BSKY: ✅

---

## UCHCs and UPHCs (Government Primary Care)

There are several **Urban Community Health Centres (UCHCs)** and **Urban Primary Health Centres (UPHCs)** located across Bhubaneswar. These provide:
*   Free OPD consultations
*   Free basic medicines (Niramaya)
*   Immunizations and maternal care
*   Referral services to larger hospitals

Check with the **Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation (BMC)** health desk for the nearest UCHC/UPHC to Niladri Vihar.
