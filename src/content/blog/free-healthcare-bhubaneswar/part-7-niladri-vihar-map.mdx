## 🏥 Tier 1: Within 5 km (Your Immediate Neighbourhood)

### 1. UPHC Chandrasekharpur (Government — FREE)
*   **Address:** Near Housing Board Colony, Chandrasekharpur, Bhubaneswar - 751016
*   **Distance from Niladri Vihar:** ~2-3 km
*   **Google Maps Tip:** Search "UPHC Chandrasekharpur Bhubaneswar" on Google Maps.
*   **Timings:** Mon-Sat, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Closed Sundays.
*   **What's FREE:** OPD consultation, basic medicines (Niramaya), immunizations, maternal check-ups, blood pressure/sugar screening.
*   **Best for:** Fever, cough, cold, routine health check-ups, childhood vaccinations, antenatal care.
*   **Important:** This is your **first stop** for any non-emergency health issue. It is the closest free facility to you. If they can't handle your case, they will refer you to Capital Hospital or AIIMS.

### 2. Kalinga Hospital
*   **Address:** Chandrasekharpur, Bhubaneswar - 751023
*   **Distance:** ~4 km
*   **Google Maps Tip:** Search "Kalinga Hospital Chandrasekharpur."
*   **Timings:** 24/7 Emergency. OPD: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Mon-Sat).
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty | **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Best for:** Emergency/accident care, general surgery, orthopaedics, gynaecology.
*   **Cost without BSKY:** OPD ₹300-500. Admission ₹5,000+ depending on treatment.
*   **Cost with BSKY:** **₹0 (Cashless)**

### 3. KIMS Hospital (Kalinga Institute of Medical Sciences)
*   **Address:** KIIT Campus, Patia, Bhubaneswar - 751024
*   **Distance:** ~5 km
*   **Google Maps Tip:** Search "KIMS Hospital Patia."
*   **Timings:** 24/7 Emergency. OPD: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Mon-Sat).
*   **Type:** Private, Super-specialty | **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Best for:** Heart disease (Cardiology & CTVS), brain/spine surgery (Neurosurgery), cancer (Oncology), kidney (Nephrology), joint replacement.
*   **Cost without BSKY:** OPD ₹500-700. Admission varies widely.
*   **Cost with BSKY:** **₹0 (Cashless up to ₹5L/₹10L for women)**

### 4. Local Polyclinics & Private Clinics (Niladri Vihar / Sailashree Vihar)
Several small clinics and nursing homes are within walking distance:
*   **Services:** OPD consultations, basic lab tests, pharmacy.
*   **Timings:** Vary. Most open 9 AM - 1 PM and 5 PM - 9 PM.
*   **Cost:** ₹300-500 per consultation. Lab tests: ₹200-1000.
*   **When to use:** Only when you need convenience (late evening, Sunday) and cannot reach a government facility.
*   **⚠️ Warning:** These are NOT free. They do NOT accept BSKY. Use government facilities or BSKY-empanelled hospitals whenever possible.

## 🔑 Special Note: For TCS Employees Who Moved from Other States

If you recently moved to Bhubaneswar for your TCS job (e.g., from Ghaziabad, UP), here's what applies to you:

### What You CAN Use:
1.  **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** — It's a Central Government hospital. Open to **ALL Indian citizens** regardless of state domicile. OPD: ₹10. Emergency: Free.
2.  **TCS Group Health Insurance** — Your employer provides health insurance (typically via MediBuddy/Medi Assist). Log into Ultimatix → Health Insurance → Check network hospitals in Bhubaneswar. Major hospitals like SUM, KIMS, Kalinga, and AMRI are usually empanelled.
3.  **Jan Aushadhi Kendras** — Open to everyone. No eligibility required. Just bring a prescription.
4.  **Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)** — Check eligibility at [beneficiary.nha.gov.in](https://beneficiary.nha.gov.in). If your family was listed in the SECC 2011 database, you may be eligible regardless of your current employment.

### What You CANNOT Use (as a non-Odisha resident):
1.  **BSKY Smart Health Card** — Restricted to permanent Odisha residents enrolled under NFSA/SFSS. As a TCS employee from UP, you are **NOT eligible**.
2.  **Niramaya (Free Medicines)** — These are for Odisha residents at state government hospitals. As a migrant, you may face restrictions. However, in practice, many government hospitals provide basic treatment to anyone who walks in. Confirm at the registration counter.
3.  **NIDAN (Free Diagnostics)** — Same as above. Technically for Odisha residents, but emergency cases are never refused.

### Your Best Strategy:
1.  **Primary care:** Use your TCS insurance at a private network hospital (cashless).
2.  **Emergency:** Call 108 (free ambulance) → Go to AIIMS (free emergency for all) or nearest private hospital (TCS insurance cashless).
3.  **Chronic medicines:** Buy from Jan Aushadhi Kendra (50-90% cheaper) to supplement your insurance.
4.  **If you obtain Odisha domicile/ration card:** You can eventually apply for BSKY benefits.
