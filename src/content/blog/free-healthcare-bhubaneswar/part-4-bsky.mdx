# Part 4: BSKY Smart Health Card — ₹5-10 Lakh Free Coverage

The **Biju Swasthya Kalyan Yojana (BSKY)** is Odisha's flagship universal health assurance scheme. If you have a BSKY Smart Health Card, you can walk into a private hospital and get treated **completely free (cashless)** up to ₹5 Lakh per year (₹10 Lakh for women).

---

## 1. What is BSKY?

BSKY is a **state government health insurance scheme** that provides:
*   **₹5,00,000** annual health coverage per family.
*   **Additional ₹5,00,000** for female members (total ₹10 Lakh for women).
*   **Cashless treatment** at government AND empanelled private hospitals.
*   **No premium** — The government pays the entire premium.

## 2. Who is Eligible?

You are automatically eligible if your family is covered under:
*   **NFSA (National Food Security Act)** ration card holders, OR
*   **SFSS (State Food Security Scheme)** card holders in Odisha.

**Important:** You do NOT need to apply online. If you have an NFSA/SFSS card, you are already enrolled.

## 3. How to Get Your BSKY Smart Health Card

If you haven't received your card yet:
1.  Visit your nearest **Mo Seva Kendra** (Government Service Centre).
2.  Carry your **NFSA/SFSS ration card** and **Aadhaar card**.
3.  The staff will verify your details and issue the card.
4.  If your card is lost, you can get a **duplicate** at the same centre.

---

## 4. What Does BSKY Cover?

### At Government Hospitals (Free for ALL):
Regardless of whether you have a BSKY card or not, **all** individuals can access free services at state government hospitals:
*   OPD, medicines (Niramaya), diagnostics (NIDAN), surgeries, ICU, inpatient care.

### At Empanelled Private Hospitals (BSKY Card Required):
*   **1,900+ medical/surgical packages** covering almost every type of treatment.
*   **Cancer treatment** (chemotherapy, radiation, surgery).
*   **Cardiac surgery** (bypass, angioplasty, valve replacement).
*   **Joint replacement** (knee, hip).
*   **Neurosurgery** (brain tumour, spine surgery).
*   **Dialysis, organ transplant, burn care**, and more.
*   **Follow-up care** for 10 days after discharge is also covered.

## 5. How to Use Your BSKY Card at a Private Hospital

1.  **Go to the BSKY Help Desk** at the empanelled private hospital.
2.  **Show your BSKY Smart Health Card** and Aadhaar.
3.  The hospital will verify your details online.
4.  Treatment is **cashless** — the hospital bills the government directly.
5.  You pay **₹0**.
