# Part 2: AIIMS Bhubaneswar — The Complete Guide

**AIIMS Bhubaneswar** is the crown jewel of healthcare in Odisha. Established under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY), it is one of the newer AIIMS institutions, located at **Sijua, Patrapada, Bhubaneswar - 751019**. It sits approximately **12 km from Niladri Vihar**.

---

## 1. How to Reach from Niladri Vihar
*   **By Auto/Cab:** ~30-40 minutes via NH-16 and Infocity Road.
*   **By Bus:** Take buses heading towards Patrapada/Sijua from Rasulgarh or Vani Vihar.
*   **Landmark:** Near Khandagiri hills, opposite Infocity.

## 2. Departments (40+ Specialties)

AIIMS Bhubaneswar has one of the most comprehensive department lists in Eastern India:

### General Departments:
General Medicine, General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Paediatrics, Ophthalmology, ENT, Dermatology, Psychiatry, Dentistry, Anaesthesiology, Emergency Medicine.

### Super-Specialty Departments:
Cardiology, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Nephrology, Urology, Gastroenterology, Endocrinology, Medical Oncology, Surgical Oncology, Radiation Oncology, CTVS (Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgery), Pulmonary Medicine, Rheumatology, Plastic Surgery & Burns, Neonatology.

### Diagnostic & Support:
Radiodiagnosis (CT, MRI, Ultrasound), Pathology, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Transfusion Medicine (Blood Bank), Nuclear Medicine, Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation (PMR).

## 3. OPD Process & Costs

### Step-by-step:
1.  **Registration:** Go to the OPD Registration Counter with your **Aadhaar Card**. Pay ₹10 registration fee (one-time, valid for 1 year).
2.  **Token/Slip:** You receive an OPD slip with a token number for your chosen department.
3.  **Consultation:** Wait for your token. The OPD typically runs from **8:00 AM to 1:00 PM** (Mon-Sat).
4.  **Investigations:** If the doctor orders tests, take the slip to the relevant diagnostic department.
5.  **Pharmacy:** Collect prescribed medicines from the AIIMS pharmacy (subsidized, not always free unless under a government scheme).

### Online Appointment Booking (Step-by-Step):

**Method 1: AIIMS Bhubaneswar Swasthya App**
1.  Download the app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
2.  Log in using your **Aadhaar-linked mobile number**.
3.  Enter the OTP received on your mobile.
4.  Tap "Appointment" → Select the department.
5.  Choose an available date and time slot.
6.  Confirm the booking. You will receive an SMS confirmation.

**Method 2: ORS (Online Registration System)**
1.  Go to [ors.gov.in](https://ors.gov.in).
2.  Select "Odisha" → "AIIMS Bhubaneswar."
3.  Click "New Appointment" or "Follow-Up."
4.  Log in with your mobile number or ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account).
5.  Select the department, date, and confirm.

**⏰ Important:** New appointment slots open at **12:00 AM (midnight)** daily. Book early to get your preferred slot.

### OPD Timings:
*   **Registration:** 7:30 AM onwards
*   **Morning OPD:** 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM (Mon-Sat)
*   **Afternoon Specialty Clinics:** Select departments offer afternoon clinics (Mon-Fri). Check the app for availability.
*   **Sundays & Public Holidays:** OPD closed. Emergency 24/7.

### AIIMS Help Desk:
*   **Phone:** 0674-2476789
*   **Mon-Fri:** 7:00 AM - 4:30 PM
*   **Saturday:** 7:00 AM - 12:30 PM
*   **Sunday/Holidays:** Closed

### Pro Tips for Visiting AIIMS:
*   **Arrive by 7:00 AM** if you don't have an online appointment. Registration queues build up fast.
*   **Carry photocopies** of your Aadhaar and all previous medical records.
*   **Carry snacks and water.** You may be there for 4-6 hours for consultation + investigations.
*   **Use online appointments** whenever possible. Walk-ins are last priority.

## 5. Honest Review

### Strengths:
*   **World-class faculty** with doctors trained at AIIMS Delhi, PGI Chandigarh, and international institutions.
*   **Modern infrastructure** with state-of-the-art equipment (256-slice CT, 3T MRI, PET-CT, Linear Accelerator for cancer).
*   **Emergency department** is excellent and handles critical trauma cases 24/7.
*   **Research-driven approach** ensures that treatment protocols are up-to-date with global standards.

### Weaknesses:
*   **Overcrowding:** Patients come from Odisha, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, and West Bengal. OPD can have 200-300 patients per department daily.
*   **Long waiting times:** Getting a surgery date can take weeks to months for non-emergency cases.
*   **Communication:** Some patients report that junior residents can be brusque due to workload pressure.
*   **Pharmacy stock:** The AIIMS pharmacy sometimes runs out of specific medicines, forcing patients to buy from outside.

### Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)
**Best for:** Complex surgeries, cancer treatment, cardiac care, neurosurgery. **Not ideal for:** Routine OPD (too crowded) or simple ailments (use a state hospital instead).

## 6. Can Non-Odisha Residents Use AIIMS?

**YES — 100%.** AIIMS is a **Central Government institution** under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India. It is open to **all Indian citizens** regardless of which state you are from.

If you are a TCS employee who moved from Ghaziabad, Delhi, or any other state:
*   **OPD:** ₹10 registration. No domicile requirement.
*   **Emergency:** Completely free. No questions asked.
*   **Investigations:** Available at subsidized rates.
*   **Surgery/Admission:** Available at subsidized rates.
*   **Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY):** If your family is in the SECC 2011 database, you can use your Ayushman card here.
*   **TCS Insurance:** AIIMS also processes insurance claims from major TPAs. Check with the billing counter.

**AIIMS is your safest option as a non-Odisha resident** because state-level schemes like BSKY, Niramaya, and NIDAN do not apply to you.
