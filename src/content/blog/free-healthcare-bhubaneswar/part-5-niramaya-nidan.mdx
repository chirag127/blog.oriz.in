# Part 5: Niramaya & NIDAN — Free Medicines & Free Diagnostics

These two schemes are the backbone of Odisha's free healthcare system. Together, they ensure that you never have to pay for medicines or diagnostic tests at a government hospital.

---

## Section A: NIRAMAYA — Free Medicines

### What is Niramaya?
Niramaya is a Government of Odisha initiative to provide **essential drugs and medicines free of cost** to all patients visiting government health facilities.

### How to Get Free Medicines (Step-by-Step)
1.  **Visit any government hospital** in Odisha (Capital Hospital, CHC, PHC, District Hospital, etc.).
2.  **Register as a patient** at the OPD counter.
3.  **Get a prescription** from the government doctor.
4.  **Go to the Drug Distribution Centre (DDC)** located inside the hospital premises.
5.  **Show your prescription** to the DDC staff.
6.  **Collect your medicines for free.** The DDC operates **24/7**.

### Who is Eligible?
*   **All permanent residents of Odisha** are eligible.
*   No income limit. No card required. Just your Aadhaar and a doctor's prescription.
*   **Exception:** Central and State government employees are NOT eligible (they have their own medical reimbursement systems).

### What medicines are available?
The DDC stocks **500+ essential drugs** covering:
*   Antibiotics, painkillers, anti-fever
*   Diabetes medicines (Metformin, Glimepiride, Insulin)
*   Hypertension medicines (Amlodipine, Losartan, Atenolol)
*   Anti-TB drugs (full DOTS course)
*   Psychiatric medicines
*   Surgical consumables and dressings

### What if a medicine is not available at the DDC?
*   Ask the DDC staff. They can sometimes arrange it from a neighbouring government facility.
*   If the drug is genuinely not in the government formulary, you may need to purchase it from a **Jan Aushadhi Kendra** (50-90% cheaper than branded) or a private pharmacy.
*   **Helpline for complaints about medicine availability:** 0674-2380608 (OSMC — Odisha State Medical Corporation).

## Section B: NIDAN — Free Diagnostic Tests

### What is NIDAN?
NIDAN is a Government of Odisha initiative to provide **free diagnostic investigations** at all government health facilities, from PHCs to Medical Colleges.

### What tests are free under NIDAN?

| Test Category | Examples | Cost to Patient |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blood Tests** | CBC, Blood Sugar, Liver Function, Kidney Function, Thyroid, HbA1c, Lipid Profile | **Free** |
| **Urine & Stool Tests** | Routine, Culture & Sensitivity | **Free** |
| **X-Ray** | Chest X-ray, Bone X-ray | **Free** |
| **Ultrasound** | Abdominal, Pelvic, Obstetric | **Free** |
| **CT Scan** | Brain CT, Chest CT, Abdomen CT | **Free** |
| **MRI Scan** | Brain MRI, Spine MRI, Knee MRI | **Free** |
| **ECG** | Standard 12-lead ECG | **Free** |
| **Echo** | 2D Echocardiography | **Free** (at major hospitals) |

### How does it work?
1.  A government doctor writes a referral/request for the test.
2.  You take it to the diagnostic department within the hospital.
3.  Many high-end tests (CT, MRI) are operated under a **PPP (Public-Private Partnership)** model — a private company operates the machine inside the government hospital, but the test is **free for the patient**.
4.  Reports are typically available within **24-72 hours** depending on the test.

---

## The Power Combo

When you combine **Niramaya** (free medicines) + **NIDAN** (free diagnostics) + **free OPD/surgery** at a state hospital, the total out-of-pocket cost for a patient is effectively **₹0**.

This is one of the most comprehensive free healthcare models in India, and it's available right here in Bhubaneswar.
