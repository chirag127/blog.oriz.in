# Part 3: Capital Hospital (PGIMER & Capital Hospital) — The State Flagship

**Capital Hospital** is the largest and most important **state government hospital** in Bhubaneswar. Located at **Unit-6, Bhubaneswar - 751001**, it is approximately **8 km from Niladri Vihar**. If you want truly free healthcare, this is your primary destination.

---

## 1. Why Capital Hospital is Special

Capital Hospital operates under the **Government of Odisha** and is the primary beneficiary of two game-changing state schemes:
*   **Niramaya** = Free Medicines
*   **NIDAN** = Free Diagnostics

This means that for an Odisha resident, virtually **everything from consultation to surgery is free**.

## 2. Departments Available

Capital Hospital has over 20 departments including:
*   **Medicine, Surgery, Orthopaedics, Gynaecology, Paediatrics, ENT, Ophthalmology, Dermatology, Psychiatry, Dental, Radiology, Pathology, Anaesthesia, Blood Bank, Physiotherapy.**
*   Specialized clinics for **Diabetes, Hypertension, TB (DOTS), Leprosy, and HIV/AIDS (ART Centre)**.

## 4. The OPD Process

1.  **Arrive early** (OPD opens at 8:00 AM). The earlier you arrive, the sooner you see a doctor.
2.  **Go to Registration Counter** — Carry your **Aadhaar Card** and any previous medical records.
3.  **Get your OPD Card** — This is free. You will be given a slip for the relevant department.
4.  **See the Doctor** — After consultation, the doctor writes a prescription.
5.  **Collect Free Medicines** — Take the prescription to the **Drug Distribution Centre (DDC)** inside the hospital. This is where Niramaya operates. You will receive your medicines for free.
6.  **Get Free Tests** — If the doctor orders blood tests, X-ray, CT scan, or MRI, go to the respective department. Under **NIDAN**, all these tests are free.

## 5. Honest Review

### Strengths:
*   **Truly free healthcare** — The Niramaya + NIDAN combination makes it arguably the best value hospital in the city.
*   **24/7 Emergency** department with trauma care.
*   **Experienced doctors** — Many senior doctors have decades of experience.
*   **ART Centre** for free HIV/AIDS treatment.
*   **Blood Bank** for free blood transfusion services.

### Weaknesses:
*   **Infrastructure is older** compared to AIIMS. The buildings are aging.
*   **Overcrowding in OPD** can be extreme, especially in Medicine and Surgery departments.
*   **Specialist availability** — Some super-specialty consultants may only be available on specific days.
*   **Cleanliness** varies by department.

### Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)
**Best for:** Free routine treatment, chronic disease management, free diagnostics, free medicines. **Not ideal for:** Super-specialty surgery (use AIIMS or BSKY private hospitals for that).
