# Part 6: BSKY-Empanelled Private Hospitals in Bhubaneswar

If you have a BSKY Smart Health Card, you are not limited to government hospitals. The Odisha government has empanelled dozens of **private hospitals** where you can get **cashless treatment** for up to ₹5 Lakh (₹10 Lakh for women).

---

## 1. IMS and SUM Hospital

*   **Location:** K-8, Kalinga Nagar, Ghatikia (~6 km from Niladri Vihar)
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty
*   **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Beds:** 1,500+
*   **Specialties:** Cardiology, Oncology, Neurosurgery, Orthopaedics, Nephrology, IVF, Trauma Centre.
*   **Review:** One of the largest private hospitals in Eastern India. Modern infrastructure with a dedicated BSKY helpdesk. Patient experience is generally good.
*   **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

## 2. KIMS (Kalinga Institute of Medical Sciences)

*   **Location:** KIIT Campus, Patia (~5 km from Niladri Vihar)
*   **Type:** Private, Super-specialty
*   **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Beds:** 1,200+
*   **Specialties:** Cardiology, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Joint Replacement, Cancer Centre.
*   **Review:** Affiliated with KIIT University. Has a strong academic and research background. The emergency department is well-organized.
*   **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

## 3. Kalinga Hospital

*   **Location:** Chandrasekharpur (~4 km from Niladri Vihar)
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty
*   **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Beds:** 500+
*   **Specialties:** General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Gynaecology, Paediatrics, ENT, Urology.
*   **Review:** Closest major private hospital to Niladri Vihar. Well-suited for emergency care and routine surgeries. Moderate pricing for non-BSKY patients.
*   **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐½ (3.5/5)

## 4. AMRI Hospital

*   **Location:** Khandagiri (~12 km from Niladri Vihar)
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty
*   **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Beds:** 300+
*   **Specialties:** Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopaedics, Gastro, Pulmonology.
*   **Review:** Part of the reputed AMRI chain. Known for quality nursing care.
*   **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

## 5. Hi-Tech Medical College & Hospital

*   **Location:** Pandara, Rasulgarh (~7 km from Niladri Vihar)
*   **Type:** Government-run Medical College
*   **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes (Government)
*   **Beds:** 700+
*   **Specialties:** All general and some super-specialty departments.
*   **Review:** Newer government medical college. Infrastructure is relatively modern. Serves as a teaching hospital.
*   **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐½ (3.5/5)

## 6. Care Hospital

*   **Location:** Bhubaneswar
*   **Type:** Private, Multi-specialty (Part of Care Hospitals Group)
*   **BSKY Empanelled:** ✅ Yes
*   **Specialties:** Cardiac Sciences, Neurosciences, Orthopaedics.
*   **Review:** Excellent cardiac care unit. Known for emergency angioplasty services.
*   **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

---

## How to Use BSKY at Any Private Hospital

1.  **Go to the BSKY Help Desk** (every empanelled hospital has one, usually near the reception).
2.  **Show your BSKY Smart Health Card + Aadhaar Card**.
3.  The desk will verify your eligibility online.
4.  **Get admitted** — the hospital sends a pre-authorization request to the BSKY portal.
5.  **Treatment is cashless.** The hospital bills the government.
6.  **Post-discharge:** Follow-up consultations for 10 days are also covered.
