# Part 9: Emergency & Ambulance Services — 108 & 102

In a medical emergency, every minute counts. Odisha has a robust, free ambulance system that can get you to a hospital quickly. Here is everything you need to know.

---

## 1. The 108 Ambulance Service (Free)

*   **Number:** Dial **108** from any phone (landline or mobile).
*   **Cost:** **Completely free.** No charges for the ambulance ride.
*   **Available:** 24/7, 365 days.
*   **Response Time:** Typically 15-25 minutes in urban Bhubaneswar.
*   **Equipped with:** Basic Life Support (BLS), oxygen cylinder, first-aid kit, stretcher. Some ambulances have Advanced Life Support (ALS) with defibrillators.
*   **Use for:** Heart attacks, strokes, accidents, severe injuries, unconsciousness, severe breathing difficulty, poisoning.

## 2. The 102 Ambulance Service (Free — Maternal & Child)

*   **Number:** Dial **102** from any phone.
*   **Cost:** **Completely free.**
*   **Purpose:** Specifically for **pregnant women** (to and from the hospital for delivery) and **sick newborns/infants**.
*   **Use for:** Labour pains, pregnancy complications, neonatal emergencies.

## 4. What to Do in an Emergency

### Step 1: Call 108
Give your **exact location** (use Google Maps if needed). Tell them the nature of the emergency.

### Step 2: First Aid
While waiting for the ambulance:
*   **Heart attack:** Make the person sit down, loosen clothing, give aspirin if available.
*   **Stroke:** Note the time symptoms started. Do NOT give food or water.
*   **Bleeding:** Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth.
*   **Unconscious:** Place in the recovery position (on their side).

### Step 3: Arrive at Hospital
The 108 ambulance will take you to the **nearest appropriate hospital**. In Bhubaneswar, this is usually AIIMS, Capital Hospital, or the nearest BSKY-empanelled private hospital.

### Step 4: Treatment Begins
*   **Government hospital:** Emergency treatment is **free**. No registration or payment is needed upfront.
*   **Private hospital (with BSKY):** Show your card. Treatment is cashless.
*   **Private hospital (without BSKY):** Treatment begins immediately (MLC cases are never refused). Payment is discussed later.

---

## 5. Odisha Police Helpline

If the emergency involves a road accident or crime:
*   **Dial 112** (Unified Emergency Helpline — connects to Police, Fire, and Ambulance).
