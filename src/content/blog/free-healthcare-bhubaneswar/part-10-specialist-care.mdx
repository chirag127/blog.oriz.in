# Part 10: Specialist & Super-Specialty Care for Free

The most expensive treatments — cancer, heart surgery, kidney dialysis, brain surgery — are exactly where free healthcare matters most. Here is how to access them in Bhubaneswar without going bankrupt.

---

## 1. Cancer Treatment

### Free/Subsidized Options:
*   **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** — Departments of Medical Oncology, Surgical Oncology, and Radiation Oncology. Has a **Linear Accelerator (LINAC)** for radiation therapy. Treatment is heavily subsidized.
*   **Acharya Harihar Post Graduate Institute of Cancer (AHPGIC), Cuttack** — The premier government cancer hospital in Odisha (~30 km from Bhubaneswar). Treatment is nearly free for BPL/BSKY cardholders.
*   **BSKY at Private Hospitals** — KIMS, SUM, and Care Hospital all offer cancer packages under BSKY. Chemotherapy, surgery, and radiation are covered cashless.

### What's covered under BSKY for cancer?
*   Chemotherapy (all cycles)
*   Radiation therapy
*   Surgical removal of tumours
*   Diagnostic biopsies, PET-CT scans
*   Post-operative follow-up

---

## 2. Cardiac (Heart) Care

### Free/Subsidized Options:
*   **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** — Full cardiac care including angiography, angioplasty, bypass surgery (CABG), and valve replacement. Subsidized rates.
*   **BSKY at Private Hospitals** — Angioplasty packages (₹1.2-1.5 Lakh), bypass surgery packages (₹2-3 Lakh), pacemaker implantation are all covered cashless.
*   **Capital Hospital** — Basic cardiac diagnostics (ECG, Echo) are free under NIDAN. Complex cases are referred to AIIMS or BSKY private hospitals.

## 3. Kidney (Nephrology) & Dialysis

### Free Dialysis:
*   **Capital Hospital** — Free dialysis for eligible patients.
*   **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** — Subsidized dialysis sessions.
*   **BSKY Private Hospitals** — Dialysis sessions are covered under BSKY packages. Most empanelled hospitals offer 2-3 sessions per week.

### Kidney Transplant:
*   Covered under BSKY at empanelled hospitals (SUM, KIMS).

---

## 4. Neurosurgery & Brain/Spine Care

*   **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** — The best option for complex neurosurgery. Has a dedicated Department of Neurosurgery with experienced faculty.
*   **BSKY** — Brain tumour surgery, spinal cord surgery, and trauma neurosurgery packages are available at empanelled private hospitals.

## 5. Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement

*   **Government Hospitals** — Fracture treatment, plating, and basic orthopaedic surgery are free at Capital Hospital and Hi-Tech Medical College.
*   **BSKY** — Knee replacement and hip replacement packages are covered at SUM, KIMS, and Kalinga Hospital. These surgeries typically cost ₹2-4 Lakh in the private market but are cashless under BSKY.

## 6. Eye Care (Ophthalmology)

*   **Capital Hospital & AIIMS** — Cataract surgery is free or heavily subsidized at both hospitals.
*   **BSKY** — Cataract surgery, retinal detachment repair, and glaucoma surgery are covered at private eye hospitals.

---

## The Strategy for Expensive Treatment

1.  **Get diagnosed** at a government hospital (free OPD + free diagnostics under NIDAN).
2.  **Check if the treatment is available** at the government hospital (AIIMS or Capital Hospital). If yes, get treated there (free or subsidized).
3.  **If not available or the wait is too long,** use your **BSKY card** to go to an empanelled private hospital (cashless up to ₹5-10 Lakh).
4.  **If the cost exceeds BSKY limits,** apply for the **Chief Minister's Relief Fund (CMRF)** or contact the hospital's **Social Welfare Officer** for additional financial assistance.
