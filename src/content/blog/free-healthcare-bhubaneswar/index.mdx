This 12-part series brings it all together in one place. After reading this, you will know **exactly** where to go, what documents to carry, and how to get treated for free or near-free in Bhubaneswar.

## 🏥 Why Bhubaneswar?

Bhubaneswar is one of the best cities in India for affordable healthcare. It has:
*   **AIIMS** (Central Government - premier institution)
*   **Capital Hospital / PGIMER** (State Government flagship)
*   **15+ BSKY-empanelled private hospitals** for cashless treatment
*   **Free medicine (Niramaya)** and **free diagnostics (NIDAN)** at all government facilities
*   **Multiple Jan Aushadhi Kendras** for generic medicines at 50-90% discount

## 🚨 Special Note: Are You an IT Employee Who Moved to Bhubaneswar?

If you are a **TCS, Infosys, Wipro, or any IT employee** who recently moved to Bhubaneswar from another state (like UP, Delhi, Maharashtra), this series has a dedicated section for you in **[Part 7](/blog/free-healthcare-bhubaneswar/part-7-niladri-vihar-map)**.

**Quick Answer:**
*   ✅ You CAN use **AIIMS Bhubaneswar** (Central Government — open to ALL Indians. OPD: ₹10. Emergency: Free).
*   ✅ You CAN use your **TCS/employer group health insurance** at private network hospitals (cashless).
*   ✅ You CAN buy cheap medicines at **Jan Aushadhi Kendras** (no eligibility needed).
*   ✅ You CAN call **108 ambulance** for free (available to everyone).
*   ❌ You CANNOT use **BSKY** (requires Odisha permanent residency + NFSA/SFSS ration card).
*   ⚠️ **Niramaya/NIDAN** are technically for Odisha residents, but government hospitals rarely turn away patients. Confirm at the registration counter.

**Your best strategy:** Use AIIMS for emergency and super-specialty care. Use TCS insurance for private hospitals. Use Jan Aushadhi for cheap medicines.
