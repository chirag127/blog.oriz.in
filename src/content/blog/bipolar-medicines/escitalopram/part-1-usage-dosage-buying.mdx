# Escitalopram: Uses, Dosage, and Buying Guide in India (Part 1)

*Medical Disclaimer: This article is for informational and educational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your psychiatrist or physician before making any changes to your medication regimen.*

Escitalopram is one of the most widely prescribed SSRI antidepressants in the world. However, when used in Bipolar Disorder, it requires immense precision. This first part covers exactly what this medicine is, how to take it, and how to navigate the Indian pharmaceutical market to buy it.

---

## Section 1: What is this medicine & how it works

### 1. Drug Class & Category
*   **Primary Class**: Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor (SSRI).
*   **Psychiatric Category**: Antidepressant / Anxiolytic.

### 2. Mechanism of Action (Simple Language)
Serotonin is the primary messenger in the brain responsible for regulating mood. Normally, once serotonin sends a message, it is vacuumed back up by the brain to be recycled. **Escitalopram blocks this vacuum.** By preventing the "reuptake," it forces more serotonin to stay in the gap between brain cells, continuously stimulating them and eventually lifting depressive symptoms.

### 3. Approved Medical Uses (Indications)
*   **Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)**: The primary indication for adults and adolescents.
*   **Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)**: Acute and maintenance treatment.

### 4. Off-Label Uses
*   **Bipolar Depression (Adjunct)**: Used to treat the depressive phase of Bipolar Disorder. It **must** be used alongside a mood stabilizer (like Sodium Valproate) to prevent the induction of mania.
*   **Panic Disorder and OCD**: Highly effective for severe anxiety spectrum disorders.

### 5. How Quickly it Starts Working
*   **Onset of Action**: The physical side effects (like nausea) begin immediately, but the psychiatric benefits (mood elevation, anxiety reduction) take **2 to 6 weeks** to manifest.

### 6. Half-Life and Duration of Effect
*   The biological half-life is **27 to 32 hours**, allowing for convenient once-daily dosing.

## Section 2: Dosage, Forms & How to Take It

### 7. Standard Adult Dose & Frequency
*   **For Depression/Anxiety**: The standard therapeutic dose is **10 mg once daily**.
*   **For Bipolar Depression**: Often started lower (e.g., 5 mg) to monitor for signs of a manic switch, before titrating up to 10 mg.

### 8. Available Forms
*   **Standard Tablets**: 5mg, 10mg, and 20mg.
*   **Combination Tablets**: Frequently combined with Clonazepam (e.g., Nexito Plus: 10mg Escitalopram + 0.5mg Clonazepam).

### 9. How & When to Take It
*   **With or Without Food**: Can be taken with or without food. If it causes nausea, taking it after a meal helps.
*   **Time of Day**: If it makes you drowsy, take it at night. If it causes insomnia, take it in the morning.

### 10. What to do if you miss a dose
*   Take the missed dose as soon as you remember. If it is close to your next dose, skip it. Never take two doses at once.

### 11. Maximum Daily Dose
*   The maximum recommended daily dose is **20 mg**. Higher doses increase the risk of heart arrhythmias without providing extra antidepressant effects.

### 12. How to Store the Medicine
*   Store at room temperature (below 30°C), away from direct sunlight and moisture.
