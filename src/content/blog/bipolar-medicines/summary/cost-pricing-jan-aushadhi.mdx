# Summary: Cost, Pricing, and Jan Aushadhi Savings

Treating Bipolar Disorder is a lifelong financial commitment. Because multiple medications are taken simultaneously every day, costs can quickly spiral. Fortunately, India offers the **Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP)**, providing world-class generic medicines at a fraction of the cost of premium brands.

Here is the ultimate cost comparison for a standard strip of 10 tablets.

---

## 1. Sodium Valproate (500mg CR/Chrono)
*   **Premium Brands**: Valparin Chrono (Sanofi), Encorate Chrono (Sun Pharma).
*   **Premium Price**: **₹90 to ₹100** per strip.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Generic**: Sodium Valproate Controlled Release Tablets BP 500mg.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Price**: **₹20 to ₹30** per strip.
*   **Savings**: ~70% cheaper.

## 2. Olanzapine (10mg)
*   **Premium Brands**: Oleanz (Sun Pharma), Olimelt (Intas).
*   **Premium Price**: **₹75 to ₹85** per strip.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Generic**: Olanzapine Tablets IP 10mg.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Price**: **₹10 to ₹20** per strip.
*   **Savings**: ~80% cheaper.

## 3. Escitalopram (10mg)
*   **Premium Brands**: Nexito (Sun Pharma), Rexipra (Intas).
*   **Premium Price**: **₹85 to ₹97** per strip.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Generic**: Escitalopram Oxalate Tablets IP 10mg.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Price**: **₹15 to ₹25** per strip.
*   **Savings**: ~75% cheaper.

## 4. Lorazepam (2mg)
*   **Premium Brands**: Lopez (Intas), Ativan (Pfizer).
*   **Premium Price**: **₹25 to ₹40** per strip.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Generic**: Lorazepam Tablets 2mg.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Price**: **₹10 to ₹15** per strip.
*   **Savings**: ~60% cheaper. *(Note: As a Schedule H1 controlled substance, generic availability may be limited at smaller centers).*

## 5. Propranolol (20mg)
*   **Premium Brands**: Ciplar (Cipla), Betacap (Sun Pharma).
*   **Premium Price**: **₹25 to ₹28** per strip.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Generic**: Propranolol Tablets IP 20mg.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Price**: **₹8 to ₹12** per strip.
*   **Savings**: ~60% cheaper.

---

### The Monthly Economic Impact

If a patient is prescribed a standard polypharmacy regimen (e.g., Sodium Valproate twice a day, Olanzapine at night, and Propranolol for tremors), the monthly cost using **Premium Brands** will exceed **₹800 to ₹1,000**. 

By switching the exact same chemical regimen to **Jan Aushadhi generics**, the monthly cost drops to roughly **₹200 to ₹300**. Over a lifetime of treatment, this represents an enormous financial relief without compromising clinical efficacy.
